Welcome to ATOM

How to play

The White Atom: Using up, down, left and right keys, navigate the white atom around attempting to collect as many protons as possible within a set time limit of 30 seconds.

Red Proton: The protons are coloured red and upon collecting them, the player gains 10 points.

Green Electrons: 3 electrons will be randomly generated onto the board and will relocate randomly whenever the white atom moves. If the user collects a green electron, 5 points will be deducted from the score.

Features: You will have 30 seconds to move the white atom around to collect as many protons as possible, without picking up any electrons to gain the highest score!
A timer counting down will be visible below the board. To the right of the timer will be a scoreboard which updates whenever the user collects a proton or a electron.
Once the game has finished, a message will appear 1 second later to the left of the timer to inform you the game has finished and your final score. A second later, a "Top 3 high score" board will appear at the bottom right of the screen(Note: You CANNOT input a name along with your scores). Following this, to the right of the top 3 scoreboard a reset button will appear where you have the option to start the game again and attempt to beat your previous scores!
